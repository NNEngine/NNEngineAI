The Core Bottleneck: Data

Regional languages face severe data scarcity with extremely limited speech datasets, low-quality recognition, poor TTS systems, and a lack of conversational data. Modern AI requires over 10,000+ hours of speech data and 100B+ tokens, preventing the development of high-quality Marathi systems.

Billion-Dollar Market Opportunity

The global AI training data market is experiencing explosive growth. Target customer segments include:

  • AI Companies: Seeking regional language models.
  • Government: Digital India initiatives.
  • Media & Telecom: Automated dubbing, IVR, customer service.
  • Accessibility: Screen readers and voice assistants.
